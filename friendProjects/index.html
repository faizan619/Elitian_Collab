Thyroid Disease Prediction

Your thyroid creates and produces hormones that play a role in many different systems throughout your body. When your thyroid makes either too much or too little of these important hormones, it's is called a thyroid Disease.There are several different types of disease, including hyperthyroidism, hypothyroidism,thyroiditis and Hashimoto's thyroiditis.
